2022 Web Design Trends Important For Everyone

AUTHOR | April 1, 2023 | 5 minute read

Web Design is always changing and growing. Some years past trends stay and some years new trends begin. What trends can we find from 2022 and are they still impacting the Web Design used in 2023?

Here is a list of trends from 2022 if you are interested in learning more:
  • Mini-sites of delight
  • Web-based scavenger hunts
  • App-like experiences
  • One-page websites
  • Read more at WebFlow.com

What exactly is Web Design?

Accoring to the Interaction Design Foundation, Web Design is defined as:

"...the design of websites that are displayed on the internet."


In other words, Web Design is the aesthetics of the website.

2022 Web Design trends

Mini-sites

Mini-sites can be a way for designers to create informal sites just to be artistic. Mini-sites typically only feature a few illustrations and sections. These sites act more like static blog-posts or single tools rather than full websites.


Mini sites can also be used as a platform for online tools. These sites benefit from using a minmial content approach to allow the visitor to really focus on the site's service.

Online Scavenger Hunts

Online scavenger hunts are exactly what they sound like! They are short little adventures that designers create mostly for entertainment.



Sometimes, these scavenger hunts are built to guide a site visitor through a narrative adventure for all different types of sites. Portfolios and one-time-use are the most commonly used site type using these scavenger hunts. Check out this awesome site for an example!
